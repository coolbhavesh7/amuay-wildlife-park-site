Student FAQ

Questions about visiting for a project.

Short, clear answers to keep you focused and prepared.

Can we drive our own cars?
No. All visitors must join official guided tours along the park’s dirt tracks. This protects habitats and keeps people safe.
Will we see every animal?
Not always. Wildlife moves freely. Use the Wildlife page to know which species are likely in each habitat.
Can we collect plants or animals?
No. Observations should be visual and written only, so that Caracas remains undisturbed for future classes.
