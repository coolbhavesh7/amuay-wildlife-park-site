A calm overview for field trip planning.

This page gathers the most useful links and ideas for teachers leading groups of students through Caracas Wildlife Park.

Before the visit

Share the Wildlife, Flora, Visit, and Conservation pages with your class so students understand what they will see and why access is controlled.

  • Set one or two clear learning goals per group.
  • Agree on behaviour rules that match park guidelines.
  • Prepare notebooks or checklists for quiet observation.
