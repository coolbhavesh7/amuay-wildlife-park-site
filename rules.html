Park rules

Key rules that protect Caracas.

A calm, minimal list you can discuss quickly with students.

Access rules

  • Only official guided vehicles may use the dirt tracks.
  • No walking away from vehicles without the guide’s permission.
  • No littering, loud music, or shouting near wildlife.

Why these rules matter

They reduce stress on animals, keep visitors safe, and prevent damage to the plants and soils that make Caracas special.
