About

A protected reserve designed for low-impact visits.

Caracas Wildlife Park is built around one simple idea: wildlife comes first. Access is controlled, routes are limited, and most learning happens through quiet observation rather than close contact.

What makes the park different

The park focuses on protecting habitats and keeping disturbance low. That’s why most visits happen as guided tours on set tracks.

  • Guided vehicles stay on designated dirt tracks.
  • Small groups keep noise down and improve safety.
  • Field notes and checklists are encouraged for learning.
