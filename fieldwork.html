Short, simple prompts to use during a visit, without extra visual clutter.
Fieldwork guide

Observe, note, and reflect.

Use these calm, structured prompts with the checklist page to guide observations without overwhelming students.

Three simple tasks

  • Choose one habitat (grassland, hill, or marsh) to focus on.
  • Record three animals and three plants you can identify or describe.
  • Write one paragraph on how rules (guided tours, no private cars) protect what you saw.
