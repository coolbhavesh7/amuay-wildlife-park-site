Moments to look for on a guided tour.

This page doesn’t use heavy images, so it stays fast and clean. Use it like a set of caption prompts—great for students building reports or presentations.

Caption prompts

Pick a few and try to “collect” them during your visit.

Simple

Dawn over marsh water

Marsh

Mist, reeds, and quiet bird calls. Describe what changes in the first 10 minutes of light.

Grassland wind lines

Plains

Look for moving patterns in grasses and how animals use open space to stay alert.

Shade on the hills

Hills

Find the boundary between sun and shade and note where insects and birds gather.

If you want to add photos later

You can drop images into the project folder and swap these cards for real photos. For now, the text prompts keep the site light and easy to print.

  • Keep wildlife distance—use zoom instead.
  • Take notes first; photos are optional.
  • Never leave the vehicle without permission.
