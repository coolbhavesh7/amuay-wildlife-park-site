Not a detailed navigation map, but a high-level picture of the three landscapes.
Map

A simple mental map of Caracas.

Use this description with your teacher’s printed map or classroom materials to visualise where each habitat sits.

Grasslands

Central

Broad plains occupy much of the park’s centre, crossed by a small number of dirt tracks used by tour vehicles.

Hills

North & West

Low hills rise to the north and west, offering viewpoints back over the plains and towards the marshes.

Marshes

Low‑lying

Marshy areas sit in the lowest parts of the park, where water collects and reeds, amphibians, and wading birds are common.
