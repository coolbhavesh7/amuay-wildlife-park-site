Contact

Questions, school visits, and tour enquiries.

Follow the contact details below to get in touch with the park’s team. We’re here to help with any questions about planning your visit, or anything else you might need. We look forward to hearing from you!

What to include in a message

  • Your preferred visit date and time window.
  • Group size and whether it’s a school group.
  • Any accessibility needs or safety concerns.

Contact options

You can reach us by email or phone, or send a message through our website’s contact form. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2 business days.
